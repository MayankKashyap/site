About Govt. college Tissa

College Name - Govt. College, Tissa

Location - Chamba, Himachal Pradesh

Address - Govt. College, Tissa, Post office Tissa, Tehsil Churah, Distt. Chamba, Himachal Pradesh - 176316

College Type - Affilated College

University Name - Himachal Pradesh University

Established in - 2006

Statutory Body - University Grants Commission

College Management - State Government

Distance - 72 km from Chamba city

Region - Rural, Snow bound

Vision

Quality Education to achieve excellence in Learning and Life.

Goal

This institute concentrates on providing education with modern means of communication - power point presentations, interactive boards,
Seminars and discussion so that an all round development of the personality of the student may be developed.
We adhere to the injunction of National knowledge Commission for appropriate development of human resources out of our students.

Mission

In the remote & difficult region of the state, the mission of this center of higher education is to inculcate a spirit of equanimity among the students.
So that dedicated citizens may come out with leadership qualities to serve the purpose of nation - building.
This is also enshrined in the insignia of college as "Sambuddhivishishyata" ie. "The person with the spitrit of equanimity is the noblest and excellent one"
