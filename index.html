Recent Activities

  • Miss. Reeta Kunari Student from the institute secures 6th rank in B.A. Final year annual exams.
  • Awareness program organised by Mr. Rajeev Thakur for stronger democracy, great responses.
  • Assistant professor Sh. Lekh Raj and Sh. Ram Nath Assistant professors of GC Tissa have attended the induction Programme from Nov 16th to 28th at Govt. College for Technical Education Dharamshala.
  • Assistant professor Dr. Vishal Walia has published a research article on "Rigidly fixed vibrations of functionally graded viscoelastic sphere" at HPU shimla in a National seminar on "New dimensions in mechanics and allied field held at 27 and 28 no 2015.
  • Dr. B.P. Badola completed 1st spell of Associateship at UGC inter university center at Indian institute of advanced study shimla for the month of august 2015.
    He delivered two invited lectures in the dept. of music and fine arts at HP university shimla on 21st aug 2015 on Metaphysical and epistemological bases of constructive knowledge" and "Exploring new dimensions of research in music and fine arts in present day concept".
    He delivered two invited lectures as resource person in the induction training at Govt. college of teacher education, Dharamshala to assistant professor on "Philosophical background of constructive knowledge" on 16th nov 2015.
  • Dr. B.P. Badola got four research articles published in the international journals in academic year 2015.
    He is working on the theme of "synchronization of indian philosophy with the post-modern thoughts in the 21st century context : Envisioning the new paradigms of modernity and post modernism".
  • The team of GC Tissa headed by Mr. Rajeev Thakur participated in a HPU intercollege youth festival held in PG college Seema, Rohru on nov 2 to 5, 2015.
